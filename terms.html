6.3 Platform Fee

A platform fee of 8% is added on top of all deposits. This fee covers payment processing (Stripe), SMS notifications, and platform operational costs. The platform fee is paid by the Client and is non-refundable.

  • Example: EUR 100 deposit + EUR 8 platform fee (8%) = EUR 108 total charged to Client
